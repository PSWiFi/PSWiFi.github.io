Welcome to Wi-Fi!

The Showdown! Wi-Fi Room is a place for players to battle, trade and connect online in the main-line Pokémon games! We currently support all 3DS and Nintendo Switch titles. The possibilities are endless!

Use the navigation bar at the top of this page to quickly jump between pages in order to find the information you need, or continue reading this page for a room overview, and to see how you can get involved!

Room Overview:
Important Information:

All Showdown rooms and trading communities have differing rulesets, so we encourage becoming familiar with the rules in order to gain an understanding of how PS! Wi-Fi operates.

We pride ourselves on being a legitimate trading community, because of this;

ALL hacks, hacking methods, and discussion of hacking is BANNED.

If you believe you have received a hacked Pokémon in a trade from someone in our community be sure to report it to an active member of Room Staff. (Found by typing /roomstaff directly into the chat!)
How To Participate:

There are a large number of ways that users can contribute towards making the room a better place and to keep the community thriving, some of these include:

  • Engaging in trades, battles & in-game discussion with people from all over the world.
  • Keeping up to date on the latest official in-game news, such as ongoing event distributions.
  • Participating in ongoing room events focused around the game to win prizes.
  • Participating in Tournaments of various tiers and rulesets.
  • Participating in Giveaways and win some rare & exclusive in-game prizes.
  • Becoming a member of the Event Helper team and working behind the scenes to bring events to life.
Supported Titles:

We support trades, battles and online communications in all officially supported main series titles, these include:

Switch:
  • Scarlet & Violet
  • Sword & Shield
  • Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl
  • Let's Go! Pikachu & Eevee
  • Legends: Arceus
3DS:
  • Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon
  • X, Y, Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire
Unsupported:
  • Pokémon HOME
  • Pokémon GO
