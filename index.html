Welcome to Wi-Fi!

Room Overview:

The Showdown! Wi-Fi Room is a place for players to battle, trade and connect online in the main-line Pokémon games! We currently support all 3DS and Nintendo Switch titles. The possibilities are endless!

Be sure to familiarize yourself with our rules. You can find them using the button at the top of the page you are currently viewing.

We pride ourselves on being a legitimate trading community. All hacks and hacking methods are banned! If you believe you have recieved a hacked Pokémon in a trade from someone in our community be sure to report it to an active member of /roomstaff.
