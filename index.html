Wi-Fi Room Overview

The Showdown! Wi-Fi Room is a community for battling, trading, and coordinating gameplay in Pokémon games on Nintendo Switch & Switch 2. We currently support the following titles:

Core Titles:
  • Scarlet / Violet
  • Sword / Shield
  • Legends: ZA / Arceus
  • Brilliant Diamond / Shining Pearl
  • Let's Go! Pikachu / Let's Go! Eevee
Side Games / Applications:
  • Pokémon Champions
  • Pokopia
  • Pokémon HOME (Friend Trades only)

This community is committed to maintaining a safe and legitimate trading and battling environment. All users must participate using only legitimate Pokémon. Trading or battling with hacked Pokémon may result in punishment.

How to Participate

You can get involved by:

  • Trading and battling with other users
  • Discussing your gameplay, game mechanics and ongoing events
  • Joining tournaments and room events
  • Host or enter room giveaways
  • Applying to become an Event Helper
