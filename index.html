Welcome to Wi-Fi!

Room Overview:

The Showdown! Wi-Fi Room is a place for players to battle, trade and connect online in the main-line Pokémon games! We currently support all 3DS and Nintendo Switch titles. The possibilities are endless!

Be sure to familiarize yourself with our rules, as we pride ourselves on being a legitimate trading community. All hacks and hacking methods are banned! If you believe you have recieved a hacked Pokémon in a trade from someone in our community be sure to report it to an active member of Room Staff. (Found by typing /roomstaff directly into the chat!)
