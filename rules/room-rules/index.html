Room Rules

Welcome to Showdown! Wi-Fi! This page contains information about our Room Rules. If you are new to PS! Wi-Fi, welcome to the community! We encourage you to read through all of the rules and become familiar with our room.

We currently facilitate trading in the following Pokémon titles:
Nintendo Switch
  • Sword & Shield
  • Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl
  • Legends: Arceus
  • Let's Go Pikachu & Let's Go Eevee
Nintendo 3DS
  • Ultra Sun & Ultra Moon, Sun & Moon
  • Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire
  • X & Y
We do not support trading in Pokémon HOME and Pokémon GO.

General Rules


The hacked Pokémon rule...

DO NOT TRADE HACKED POKEMON IN THE WI-FI ROOM!

Trading self-hacked Pokémon, including those that come from misuse of third-party tools or applications will be evaluated by staff on a case-by-case basis.
    - If we believe this was an act of carelessness or ignorance, you will be met with a blacklist with the chance to appeal 6 months from the date of the blacklist.
    - If we believe this was an act of malice, you will be met with a blacklist with no chance to appeal, this will last 12 months from the date of the blacklist.
Trading any hacked Pokémon once will result in a roomban in most cases, and multiple counts of trading hacked Pokémon will result in more severe punishments, weekban or blacklist. This rule extends to knowingly trading suspicious Pokémon from outside sources that are found to be hacked.

Do not offer, ask for or discuss hacking in Wi-Fi or in private messages. If you are found to be trading Pokémon you have edited/created in any way, you will be punished with a room blacklist for self-hacking Pokémon. If you have been offered a hacked Pokémon from a user in the room, be sure to report it to a member of /roomstaff immediately!

The scammer rule...

Scamming once leads to a blacklist, and multiple counts of scamming will result in a permanent blacklist. A scam is defined by a trade that did not meet the agreement between both concerned parties. For example, lying about IVs or Pokémon origin, conducting a giveaway and not giving the winner their Pokémon is also considered a scam. This rule is straight and narrow, do not attempt to scam anybody.

The poor presence rule...

If you have received a blacklist for exceptionally poor behavior and/or presence you will not be given the opportunity to appeal. If you have sufficient evidence to suggest your blacklist was unjustified, contact a Room Owner. Use /roomstaff to check for an active Room Owner.

Disclaimer: The final verdict of any moderation action will always remain at the discretion of Wi-Fi /roomstaff.

Click here for our Blacklist appeal form.

General chat rules:

  1. Read through and follow the Global Rules when participating in discussions on the site.
  2. Do not discuss moderation actions in the chat. If you have a question or concern about a staff action, contact a member of /roomstaff.
  3. Do not spam, flood, or abuse formatting. Avoid abusing CAPS, Bold, Italics, >Greentext, etc.
  4. You may post on-topic links. Add a caption explaining what is inside the link.
  5. Refrain from discussing controversial / inappropriate topics. This includes but is not limited to topics such as politics, religion, race, gender, or sexuality. This is a Pokémon chatroom, keep it friendly, keep it fun. This rule extends to making rude or inappropriate comments towards ongoing Giveaways, this will not be tolerated.
  6. Do not advertise for other servers, channels, tournaments, giveaways, streams, etc. unaffiliated with Showdown or Smogon. You may ask people to PM you to join a league; however do not post any links in the room.
  7. You cannot clone a Pokémon for another user without being a member of the approved cloners team. This includes “Keeping one for yourself” or “Sending back the original”. Do not attempt to circumvent cloning rules.
  8. Do not host giveaways of disallowed Pokémon or services. See the Giveaway Rules for more information.
  9. No spoilers. Try to keep the room free from story spoilers for 1 month after the release of a new game, unless otherwise stated. This does not mean head over to Pokémon Games to post spoilers. This room also enforces spoiler rules which may be different than ours; be mindful of different rooms having different rules.
Hacking discussion includes, but is not limited to:
  1. External tools or applications.
  2. In-game glitches.
  3. Internal or external modification of cartridges.
  4. Use of modified ROMs.
  5. Exploits used to defy game mechanics, such as AltWFC.
Hacking Pokémon includes, but is not limited to:
  1. Any edits to Pokémon data using glitches or third party tools and applications.
  2. Modifications to save file and RNG seeds that create impossible results or render the Pokémon/Save file illegitimate.
  3. Internal or external modification of cartridges.
  4. Incorrect Wonder Card injection.
