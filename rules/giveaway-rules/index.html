Giveaway Information

This page contains important information about the Giveaway rules. Read through all of the information carefully if you plan to participate in joining or hosting Giveaways in the room.

What are Giveaways?

Giveaways are a way for users in Wi-Fi to give Pokémon back to the community in Sword/Shield or Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl. We have two types of giveaways: Lotto and Question.

Lotto - These are luck based, click join and hope for the best.

Question - These are skill based, test your knowledge and win some amazing prizes!

Pokémon in our Giveaways are usually considered high value or high quality, if you are a collector we advise you turn on Giveaway notifications so you don't miss any. This can be done by typing /rfaq ga into Wi-Fi chat and clicking the "Notify" button! This will ensure you are pinged as and when new Giveaways occur.

Remember that all Giveaways hosted by users in the room are done through acts of kindness and are generally unscheduled, so please do not ask questions regarding when the next Giveaway will be, and avoid complaining if you don't manage to win a desirable Giveaway.

We currently support Official Giveaways in the following Pokémon titles:

Scarlet & Violet - Indicated by the RED background

Sword & Shield - Indicated by the BLUE background

Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl - Indicated by the PURPLE background

Which Pokémon can be given away?

Your Pokémon must meet the following minimum requirement to be given away:

  • Shiny Pokémon of any kind
  • Event Pokémon of any kind
  • Pokémon with transfer exclusive movesets
  • Non-shiny flawless Pokémon
  • Non-shiny battle ready Pokémon - (Must be HyperTrained if IVs are not ideal for competitive play)

The following types of Pokémon can be given away unofficially:

  • Flawed breedjects with high IVs (Example: Mudkip, Adamant, 31/31/x/31/31/31)
  • Physical event distribution codes
  • Non Shiny Pokemon with less than 5IVs obtained in Legends: Arceus

An unofficial Giveaway means you can make a post in the room to explain what you are giving away, and how a user can recieve their freebie.

The following types of Pokémon cannot be given away through any means:

  • Anything not listed above
  • Hacked or illegal Pokémon
  • Non-self obtained Pokémon
  • Non-shiny flawless Pokémon
  • Non-shiny battle ready Pokémon - (If the IVs are not optimal for competitive play and the prize is not Hyper Trained)

We have a special protocol for those who wish to Giveaway hacked/genned Pokémon, be sure to PM a member of /roomstaff and let us know your intentions.

Disclaimer: The final verdict on if a Pokemon can be given away ultimately falls at the discretion of PS! Wi-Fi Room Staff.

Giveaway Rules

Wanting to host your own Giveaway or to see how you can participate? This portion of the page covers everything you need to know.

1. You may only give away legitimate, self-obtained Pokémon.

See the Trade Rules page for more information about the types of Pokémon that are considered legitimate.

Only self-obtained Pokémon can be given away, do not give away someone elses Pokémon. If you did not capture, or redeem the Pokémon on your console then it is not self-obtained. The only exception to this rule is the monthly Official Giveaway.


2. If your Pokémon needs proof to trade it needs proof to be given away.

See the Proofing Guidelines for more information about the types of Pokémon that need proof to trade in the Wi-Fi room.

Got the correct proofing? Simply add a link to it inside the Other Information box before submitting your Giveaway form.

The Other Information box should otherwise only be used to provide additional information about your Pokémon.


3. You cannot Giveaway a Pokémon in an egg.

This is to prevent risk of scams as the player is unable to verify their prize during the trade.


4. No inappropriate/offensive trainer names.

Your trainer name must not reference anything of an inappropriate or offensive nature. The solution to this is using a different save file with a more appropriate trainer name.


5. Held items are considered an extra, not the main prize.

If you plan to give away an item, it must be attached to a Pokémon that meets the minimum giveaway requirements listed at the top of this page.


6. Question giveaways must be Pokémon related.

You must also provide the source of your answer to a /roomstaff member in order to verify your Giveaway.


Hosting Elegibility:
  • Your account must be autoconfirmed on the site. This is achieved by having an account registered for over a week and winning a rated battle.
  • You must promptly give the prize to the winner (Within 30 minutes). For this reason, ensure that you have plenty of time to spare before hosting one.
  • Only generally well behaved members of the community can host giveaways. Poorly behaved users, particularly those with involved with hacked Pokémon and scams cannot host Giveaways. This also extends to major behavioural issues at staff discretion.
  • You cannot host Giveaways if you have an existing Giveaway ban for breaking the rules. These last for a whole week.
Participation Elegibility:
If you're simply looking for information about how you can participate in Giveaways you're in the right place:
  • Your account must be autoconfirmed on the site. This is achieved by having an account registered for over a week and winning a rated battle.
  • Do not double dip Giveaways. Do not join a Giveaway of a Pokémon you have already won unless otherwise stated by the Giveaway host.
  • Do not make negative comments. This is unwanted and will result in a swift Giveaway ban.
  • You cannot join a Giveaway if you have an outstanding room punishment.

How to host your own Giveaway

This portion of the page will cover how you can host your own Giveaway with picture examples to follow.

Step 1: Type /giveaway submit and decide which type of giveaway you'd like to host.

Step 2: Create your Pokémon on Teambuilder and click Import/Export, then copy to clipboard.

Step 3: Fill out the information in the giveaway form and click "Submit".

Step 4: Wait for a staff member to approve your giveaway or contact you for some more information. Have fun! Thank you for being charitable!
