Trade Rules

This page contains important information about the trade rules. Read through all of the information carefully if you plan to participate in trading in the room.

We facilitate trading in all main series Pokémon games that are currently supported by official Nintendo Online services. This includes all 3DS and Switch titles.

Reminder: TRADING HACKED OR ILLEGAL POKEMON IS STRICTLY BANNED.

1. You must provide any information about a Pokémon if asked by a member of Staff.

In the event a member of /roomstaff contacts you in regards to a room trade, you are obligated to provide the information requested.


2. You must be able to provide proof for all transactions if proof is required.

See the Proofing Requirements page for more information about the types of Pokémon that need proof to trade. Proofing is generally reserved for high value Pokémon to help certify legitimacy.

Proof should come from the person trading the high value Pokémon. The original owner of the Pokémon is not obligated to provide the proofing on request.

It is recommended to save proof links you receive from trades in the room if you wish to further trade the Pokémon around. Avoid asking for proof links you did not save.


3. You may only trade in-game Pokémon, items, or services.

Trading Pokémon for money, possessions, or event distribution codes is banned. This is to prevent risk of scams.

Event distribution codes can be given away for free if you wish on a first-come-first-served basis. You may also ask users to redeem event codes on your behalf, but be cautious of the potential scam risk involved in doing so.


4. Limit your trade posts to a singular message.

Once you have found a trade partner you can take the trade discussion to private messages. Try to keep trade posts once per 20 lines to reduce clutter.


5. Tag your trade post to make people aware of the game you are trying to trade in.

Use tags such as [Gen 6] [Gen 7] [LGPE] [SwSh] [BDSP] [PLA]. We also recommend using a LF (Looking For) and (For Trade) format when making a trade post.

Example: LF: Hidden Ability Bulbasaur / FT: Hidden Ability Charmander or Squirtle [SWSH]


6. Multiple-for-one, GTS & HOME trades are discouraged.

This is to prevent risk of scams, multiple-for-one trades are not strictly banned, but be aware of the scam risk involved with accepting these types of trade deals.

Trading within Pokémon HOME is unsupported by PS! Wi-Fi, do so at your own risk. It's recommended to trade within official main series games where possible.

Regarding trade evolutions, and training services:

You may offer to evolve or train a Pokémon for other users but should come to an agreement on the expected timeframe that the transation/deal will be completed. If no timeframe is agreed in private between both parties, Room Staff may intervene upon receiving a report about an incomplete trade after 24 hours has passed.

If you are offering a reward to a player in return for evolution or training services, ensure your reward is ready to trade before making the deal.

If you are the one evolving or training a Pokémon for somebody else, ensure you have the resources to do so before making the deal. Keep the person you are trading with well informed and remain in regular contact to avoid these types of trade deals being treated as scams.


7. WC injections and save restores are permitted if performed correctly.

Wonder Card injection is permitted under strict conditions, you must inject the card properly and be absolutely certain of this. You will be held responsible for illegal Pokémon as a result of an incorrectly injected Wonder Card.

If the Wonder Card is injected properly this is considered okay by PS! Wi-Fi trading standard, as the end result is no different to having legitimately obtained the event distribution from a code or location.

Save restores are permitted under the condition that it does not produce an illegal result, such as save restoring over a story altering choice.

Example of permitted use: Using a save restore to catch a legendary more than once.

Example of banned use: Using save restores to capture Regidrago when Regieleki has already been caught on the same save file.


8. All forms of seed injection are strictly BANNED in this room.

If you are caught using seed injections, you will be heavily punished. PS! Wi-Fi has a 0 tolerance for seed injection offenses.


9. Do not attempt to expose someone in the chat for a trade rule violation.

Report the violation to a member of /roomstaff and we will handle it accordingly. Do not post public blacklists.


10. Once you trade a Pokémon away you lose control over it.

Do not attempt to control what a user does with a Pokémon you have traded to them. It is their Pokémon now and the Pokémon you traded for is yours.


11. All trades initiated on Showdown must be completed on-site.

Trades must be fully completed within the Wi-Fi room if they were initiated in the Wi-Fi room. Any attempt to take trades to different platforms will be viewed as an attempt to circumvent the Trade Rules. These cases will be reviewed by Room Staff and may result in punishment.


12. Do not offer unapproved cloning services.

The only users that are permitted to clone Pokemon for others are the members of the approved cloners team. You may face room punishment if you are found to be offering cloning services to users and are not on list of approved cloners.


Contact a member of Room Staff in private messages if you have any questions or concerns about any of the Trade Rules.

Can I trade this Pokémon?

Whether you can trade a Pokémon or not depends on how the Pokémon was obtained, and how it ended up in your hands. Find the situation that best applies to you in the list below:

I caught, claimed or hatched it myself. ✅

Pokémon obtained through regular gameplay on an official, unmodified game can be traded in the room. Be sure to check the Proof Guidelines to see if it needs proof.

RNG abuse is also permitted as this simply takes advantage of understanding how the game generates the details of the Pokémon in order to achieve a desirable result.

Cloning is also permitted if done properly, properly cloned Pokémon are no different to the original entity.


Someone traded it to me/I won a Giveaway in the Wi-Fi room, it came with proof. ✅

Chances are this Pokémon can be traded, contact a member of Room Staff if you are uncertain/suspicious about a Pokémon you have received in room trades or giveaways.


It is (or was hatched from) a flawless shiny egg from a Link Trade in Gen 8/9. 😐

You may NOT trade this Pokémon in the room. Flawless shiny Pokémon originating from Link Trades are a large source of hacks. As a result there is a blanket ban on all flawless shiny eggs obtained in link trades in Generation 8 & 9 games.

If your egg is self-obtained, then make sure to hatch the Pokémon inside before trading it around.

This rule also extends to flawless (6IV) Manaphy in Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl.


It is a rare event from Gen 3. 😐

The only event Pokémon from generation 3 that can be traded are those that come from items, such as Old Sea Map Mew and Pokémon encountered from boat tickets. Otherwise, these Pokémon cannot be traded in the room.


I altered the length of a chained Pokémon to achieve a result faster. 😐

Altering the length of chains is permitted as long as this does not alter the generation of Pokémon in any way. If your chain was achieved naturally or via RNG abuse, this rule does not apply to your Pokémon.

If you have altered the chain length to achieve a result, this should be noted during the trade to make sure the receiver is aware. This can be noted in private messages, on trade sheets, or in Giveaway forms.


The Pokémon has a Jumbo or Mini mark in Gen 9. 😐

Only wild-caught Pokémon that qualify for the Jumbo/Mini mark can be traded freely in the room.

The following Jumbo/Mini mark Pokémon are banned from trading:

  • Shiny Pokémon from Tera Raids or Egg encounters
  • Any Pokémon from Mystery Gift, Static, or in-game gift encounters (These are all shiny forced or shiny locked as of present)

The Pokémon was cloned using an unaffiliated Sysbot. 😐

Cloning using Sysbots is permitted, under the assumption that the internal details of the Pokémon are not altered in any way as a result of using one.

If you are found to be cloning using "bad" Sysbots that alter Pokémon data, you may be punished as a result of doing so. PS! Wi-Fi are not affiliated with any bot hosts!

Consider using our approved cloning service instead, or speak with the owner of the bot before using it to avoid creating unintentionally hacked Pokémon.


This Pokémon was obtained by an in-game glitch or exploit. ❌

Any Pokémon obtained by glitches that alter Pokémon data are banned from trading in the Wi-Fi room, this includes but is not limited to:

  1. 8F, Coin Case and Mimic exploits in Virtual Console.
    2. This includes: Any Pokémon chat was obtained via any of the above mentioned glitches, ALL Pokémon obtained this way are considered illegal/hacked, no exceptions.

  2. Any form of Arbitrary Code Execution (ACE) abuse in any other game.
    3. This includes: Any Pokémon chat was obtained via Arbitrary Code Execution abuse, ALL Pokémon obtained this way are considered illegal/hacked, no exceptions.

  3. Glitches or exploits that result in otherwise unreleased/unobtainable Pokémon.
    4. This includes but is not limited to: Void Glitches (Diamond/Pearl), Union Circle exploits (Scarlet/Violet).

  4. Pokémon from fangames, modified game cartridges and event distribution cartridges.
    5. This includes: Any Pokémon obtained via the above mentioned methods, these are considered to be obtained by unofficial means and are therefore banned from trading in any capacity.

  5. Pokémon obtained from Menu Glitches in Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl.
    6. This includes: Any Pokémon chat was obtained via menu glitching, ALL Pokémon obtained this way are considered illegal/hacked, no exceptions.

Cute Charm abuse is permitted. This is taking advantage of standard TID/SID RNG abuse, which is a normal game mechanic and so Pokémon that were obtained as a result of Cute Charm abuse are considered legitimate.


I got it from a streamer, stranger on Y-Comm/PokePortal, Wonder/Surprise Trade, or GTS/GWS. ❌

You may NOT trade this Pokémon in the room. The majority of "good" Pokémon from these places are hacked. Trading these Pokémon will result in a ban.


I got it from a friend/relative, a used game, eBay, or another trading community. ❌

Do not trade Pokémon purchased from other websites here as most of them will end up being hacked. If you plan to trade Pokémon that have been obtained from another community, do so at your own risk as you will be held responsible if they come to our attention as being hacked Pokémon.


A user gave me legendaries, shinies, etc. for free in PMs. ❌

Inform a member of Room Staff immediately! If a deal sounds too good to be true, it probably is. There is a high chance that these types of Pokémon will turn up hacked.

Do not ask or bait users into trading you suspicious Pokémon, it is permitted to go through with an offered trade of a suspicious Pokémon if your intention is to inform a member of Room Staff afterwards.

Unofficial Giveaways are also strictly banned. All Giveaways are required to follow the Giveaway Rules.


I genned or hacked this Pokémon. ❌

Please let staff know that you are trying to trade a self-hacked Pokémon so you can be banned from the room forever. Thanks!
