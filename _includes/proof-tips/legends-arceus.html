Proofing Tips

Legends: Arceus does not have an IV judge, this means you will need prove the IVs a different way, alternatives include:

  • Viewing Grit Level values immediately after capture (Grit Level 3 = IV value of 31)
  • Pokémon HOME IV evaluation
  • Output from an RNG manipulation tool
  • Third party tools/applications if you have access to them (for example, an overlay)

It is also recommended to have a spare space in your party for the Pokémon to be placed into, this will ensure that you have plenty of time to take a proof video.
