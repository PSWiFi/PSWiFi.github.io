Important Information

This card contains the step-by-step process to follow in order to communicate with the bot properly. Read through the ALL of the information here before asking questions about how the bot operates.

The bot searches for players on a link code, then distributes a Pokémon to the player which corrisponds to the nickname of the Pokémon shown to the bot. Heres how it's done!

How to request a Pokémon:
  1. Capture any Pokémon you can nickname.
  2. Look up the nickname in the tables below for the Pokémon or Item you want.
  3. Nickname the Pokémon accordingly!
  4. Link trade on code 3910 0000 until you find IGN: Anubis.
  5. Show the bot the Pokémon with the correct nickname, then accept & confirm the trade. The bot will quit on you if you offer something else.
  6. Trade complete! You can now leave the trade.
Rules & Etiquette:
  1. Avoid contacting the Giveaway host (Anubis) with questions, requests or anything to do with bot uptimes.
  2. For everyone else's sanity, avoid asking if the bot is currently active/running. Wi-Fi staff do not keep 24/7 tabs of this information. If you are having trouble matching with the bot then try again later.
  3. As stated above, this bot has a primary focus of providing users with Pokémon and Items to assist with upcoming promotional raids, do not expect the bot to be up constantly.
  4. Leave the trade promptly after receiving your Pokémon as there may be others waiting in line!
