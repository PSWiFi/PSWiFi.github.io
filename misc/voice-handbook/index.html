Room Voice Handbook

Congratulations and welcome, Room Voices! This document will serve as a reference point for everything you need to know about your role as a Room Voice, from commands, to examples of things we like to see.

Important - Avoid sharing this page outside of the #voices channel. We are aware that the repository for this site is public, but going out of your way to share this page to people who are not on the voices team will be considered leaking.
Good practice of helpful commands!

Your role as voice helps you to assist more users as you can now publicly broadcast information such as roomfaqs (rfaq commands). You can see a full list of Wi-Fi rfaqs by simply typing /rfaq into the room. We love to see voices helping users where they can!

Example Scenario 1:

TypicalUser123: How do I host a lottery giveaway?

+ExceptionalVoice: !rfaq GA

+ExceptionalVoice: Take a look through the information here, RandomUser123, and be sure to visit the Giveaway Rules.

TypicalUser123: Thank you for the help!

Providing the user with everything they need to know in a friendly and approachable manner is key. Try to keep responses as clear as possible and reference rfaqs and/or pages of the website where applicable.

Example Scenario 2:

BadUser456 was banned from the Wi-Fi room by Billo. (Trading hacked Pokemon)

+ExceptionalVoice: So what Pokemon/set did you guys use to beat the recent event raids? I need suggestions.

TypicalUser123: I used a Cosmog with 252 Atk 252 Speed and Splash for maximum damage output.

Hangman can be used to divert attention from a moderation action, when done sparingly; hangman spam can be disruptive to current room discussion. Instead, we recommend using a more naturally formed solution such as those provided in the above examples.

Example Scenario 3:

TypicalUser123: I'm bored, what is there to do in this room?

+ExceptionalVoice: !events

+ExceptionalVoice: You can participate in any of the currently ongoing events and even get prizes for them!

It's always good to direct users looking for something to do to our events as that promotes them wanting to be a part of the room and gives them a fun experience at the same time.

Hosting your own giveaways!

First and foremost giveaways are always appreciated but by no means mandatory! They are a good way to accrue excitement in the room or redirect attention away from unwelcoming, such as after a moderation action. Giveaways can be as simple as a flawless Breedject or as far out as a flawless Shiny, your generosity has no limit! Remember as a voice, staff member approval for your Giveaway is not required, you can just get right into it!

Be sure to include the relevant proofing in the Extra Information of your Giveaway. (Only if your Pokemon needs proof to trade according to our Proof Guidelines).

Helpfulness without commands!

You don't always have to rely on using commands, using your knowledge of in-game mechanics to answer general questions a user may have is also a great thing to do! If you do not have an immediate answer but would like to help a user in the room, try referencing reliable sources like Bulbapedia (for general game information) or Pikalytics (for competitive team building and strategies).

Example Scenario 4:

TypicalUser123: Does anyone know where I can find the colored shards in PLA?

+ExceptionalVoice: You can get them randomly from Space Time Distortions on the ground.

TypicalUser123: Oh perfect, thanks!

You do not always need to point people to internet sources if the answer is short and you are confident that the advice you are giving is correct.

Example Scenario 5:

TypicalUser123: What is a good set for swampert for SwSh in-game single battles?

+ExceptionalVoice: Yawn, Flipturn, Stealthrock, Earthquake all seem to be Swamperts most used moves, check here for more info about other potential options.

TypicalUser123: Sweet, thanks!

Remember to give your links a caption/description, this is so the people reading the chat can have an idea of what is inside before clicking!

Example Scenario 6:

TypicalUser123: Can anyone help me with a trade evo?

+ExceptionalVoice: Sure, DM me!.

TypicalUser123: Sweet, thanks!

Being helpful can also extend past answering questions such as helping with Trade Evolutions and the likes. This is extremely helpful to prevent the risk of scams, Voices and higher ranking are seen as the rooms trusted users.

Leaving feedback!

We always appreciate feedback and insight into the room if you notice any problems or concerns, feel free to bring it up in the #voices channel or directly to any member of /roomstaff. We are more than happy to hear about what we can do to make things better if we can. This can also simply extend on how to possibly do things better that we may already be doing!

Example Scenario 7:

+ExceptionalVoice: I noticed users saying (asking about/doing) X, Y, Z. Maybe we could try to do A, B , C (or is this something to be worried about, I'm not too certain about these things).

Example Scenario 8:

+ExceptionalVoice: Hey CoolStaffMember, I've noticed (Issue/Concern) is happening a lot. Do you think there's an issue with this (or maybe something that could be done about it)?.

Summary

Every example listed above are things we love to see from you, keep in mind you are under no obligation to do any of these things, simply continue being yourself! If you feel like you want to contribute in similar ways to those listed above, go for it! Extra help is always welcomed. Do your best to provide the most accurate information you can when assisting a user as we like to avoid the passing of misinformation. Check your sources or ask questions in the #voices channel of the server if you need anything double checking with a member of staff. We do not expect you to know everything!
