Online Communications:

Trades & Friendly Battles:
  1. In game, press [Y] to enter the Y-Comm menu.
  2. Press [+] to connect to the internet if you are not online.
  3. Select [Link Trade] or [Battle] from the menu.
  4. Select [Set Link Code].
  5. Enter an 8-digit code that both you and your trade partner have decided.
  6. Keep selecting [A] until you exit the Y-Comm.
  7. Wait for your trade to start.
Max Raid Battles & Dynamax Adventures:
  1. In game, press [Y] to enter the Y-Comm menu.
  2. Press [+] to connect to the internet if you are not online.

  3. IF YOU ARE RAID HOST, head over to your raid den and interact with it.
  4. Press [+] to set an 8-digit link code, this will be used by participants to join your raid.
  5. Select [Invite Others]
  6. Once all the participants have joined the raid, select [Ready to Battle!].
  7. The raid will now start.

  8. IF YOU ARE A PARTICIPANT, wait for the host to inform you that their raid is seeking available to join.
  9. Once the raid is accepting joiners, Press [Y] on the Y-Comm menu to refresh the stamps.
  10. Locate the raid on the list, once found, click in it and select [Join in]
  11. Enter the decided link code, then select [Start a Challenge] to join the party
  12. Ready up and wait for the host to start the raid.

Other:

Date Skipping:

The term "Date Skipping" is used to describe the act of resetting in-game time based events instantly by taking advantage of a game exploit. All in-game time based events will reset if a date skip is performed successfully.

In Sword/Shield, Date Skipping also serves as a method to advance the RNG state of Max Raids and overworld based encounters. (Find out more about RNG abuse an our official Discord server)

  1. Go into the raid menu and Select [Invite Others].
  2. While the game is searching, Press the [HOME] button.
  3. Go to [System Settings], scroll down to [System] then select [Date and Time]
  4. Change the date forward by 1, then select [OK].
  5. Close the settings menu and re-open the game.
  6. Cancel the raid search and leave the den.

Online Communications:

Trades & Friendly Battles:
  1. In game, head downstairs in any Pokecentre at least one time; doing this once will allow you to access the union room without having to go to the Pokecenter every time.
  2. Press [Y] to enter the communications menu.
  3. Select [Global Room] from the menu.
  4. Select [Yes, with a Link Code] then save the game.
  5. Enter an 8-digit code that both you and your trade partner have decided.
  6. Keep selecting [A] until you enter the union room.
  7. Decide which one of you will be initiating the trade, trade initiator should select [Y] at this point, then [Call], then [Trade] OR [Battle]
  8. The trade partner should then interact with the host, and both players select [A] until a trade has begun.

Online Communications:

In Legends: Arceus, the only way to communicate with other players interactively is by trading. Follow the guide below to learn how to trade:

  1. Head back to to Jubilife HQ, and walk towards the Training Dojo until you see a stall.
  2. Interact with the NPC stood next to the stall.
  3. Once you have made it through the text boxes, enter an 8 digit link code to begin searching for a trade
  4. Let your trading partner know the link code and wait for them to join you!

Obtaining Competitive Pokemon

Wanting to learn how to obtain Pokemon suitable for competitive battles? This section will provide information about everything you need to know.

Includes: IV Information, Breeding Guides, Useful NPC locations.

About IVs

IVs are equivalent to genes for each Pokémon. Each Pokémon has 6 IVs based on the six main stats: HP, Attack, Defense, Special Attack, Special Defense, Speed. A Pokemon with 0 IVs in speed will be slower than an identical Pokemon with > 0 speed IVs. These values can only be altered on existing Pokemon via Hyper Training with bottle caps.

The value of an IV can range between 0-31, the goal for obtaining competitive Pokemon is to obtain the perfect value you need to have the best possible version of your desired Pokemon for competitive battles.

Typically a perfect IV is either 31 or 0, 31 to maximize the potential of a stat and 0 to bring it to it's minimum. Usage examples include having low speed for trick room Trick Room or low attack to take minimum damage when hit with a move like Foul Play.

Best methods to obtain high IV Pokemon:
  • Taking advantage of breeding mechanics is considered the fastest and most effective method towards obtaining Pokemon with flawless IV spreads.
  • In Sword/Shield, Max Raid battles with high star count, Dynamax Adventure raids, and Wild Brilliant Aura Pokemon are guaranteed to have multiple perfect IVs.
  • In Legends: Arceus, Massive Mass Outbreaks and Wild Alpha Pokemon are guaranteed to have multiple perfect IVs.
  • In Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl, Let's Go Pikachu/Eevee & 3DS Titles, you can obtain Pokemon with multiple perfect IVs through the various chaining methods available in each game.

In Sword/Shield and Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl, you can check IVs after unlocking the IV judge function, this is done by opening your boxes, hovering over the Pokemon you want to check, then pressing the [+] button

In Legends: Arceus there is no way to battle with friends, and IVs are instead based on Effort Levels. The Effort Level values your Pokemon has upon initial capture will represent it's actual IVs when transferred to another main series title. This value can be seen any time in the Pokemon summary screen.

Competitive Breeding Guides & Tips

Breeding is the most commonly used method used by players to obtain competitive viable Pokemon quickly and efficently. There are various different mechanics you need to learn to make the most of this feature. We encourage looking through the Smogon Official Breeding Guide if you wish to learn more.

Pokemon obtained through breeding can pass the ball, nature, ability, unique egg moves and up to 5 of the IVs of a parent Pokemon. This means through efficent gameplay you can obtain competitive Pokemon in a flash!

Shiny Odds Resources

This section of the page includes resources for shiny odds in 3DS and Switch titles along with information about the Star/Square shiny rates.

Shiny Odds

Shiny odds widely vary depending on your situation and the game you are hunting in.

In Generation 1-5:
  • The base shiny odds are 1/8192. This applies to all types of encounters in the game.
  • Generation 5 introduced Shiny Charm and Masuda Method breeding.
In Generation 6-8:
  • The base shiny rate is 1/4096, increased to 1/1365 with the Shiny Charm.
  • Masuda Method breeding is 1/683, increased to 1/512 with the Shiny Charm.
  • Dynamax Adventure Pokémon have a base shiny rate is 1/300, increased to 1/100 with the Shiny Charm.
  • Brilliant Aura Pokémon can have boosted 1/455 shiny rates by adapting the KO method.
Unaffected by Shiny Charm (Switch): Sword/Shield:
  • Gift Pokémon
  • Regina NPC
  • Max Raids
  • All Regis
  • Static Encounter Impidimp/Morgrem from Glimwood Tangle
Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl:
  • Every non-egg encounter, only eggs from the nursery are affected by the Shiny Charm.
Legends: Arceus:
  • Everything unaffected by the Shiny Charm cannot be shiny in Legends: Arceus. This includes all Legendary, Mythical and Scripted encounters.
