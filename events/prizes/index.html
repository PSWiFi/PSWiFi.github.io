Prizes Participation Guidelines:

Want to understand how the Prize Shop works? You're in the right place! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of the Prizes section and outlines everything you need to know in order to make a purchase! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information.

Wi-Fi Points (WP) are style of currency we have in Wi-Fi, you can use it to spend on valuable Pokemon and items in our Prize Shop! WP is earned through participation in Small Events and Tournaments throughout the month, build up your WP count as high as you can and buy those rare prizes... Or some Master Balls if you prefer.

Prize Shop Rules:

General Rules:

  1. All sales are final! You cannot refund a WP purchase once it has been delivered.
  2. Pokémon listed in the prize shop will not come with proof. This means you cannot trade the ones that need proof in the room as they are intended to be rare and exclusive prizes.
  3. Please allow up to two weeks for your order to be delivered. We do try to deliver as promptly as we can!

Important Information:

  • You can request a custom Pokémon for the prize shop by using this form.
  • Any questions or concerns about the prizes section can be directed to Nol, Laguna & SexyMalasada.
