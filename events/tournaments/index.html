Tournaments Participation Guidelines:

Want to Participate in tours? Awesome! This page contains information about Tournament rules and relevant information outlining everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators. .

Battle & compete to be the very best, grab your Pikachu and stomp through your competitors! Our Tournaments allows our users to compete for awesome prizes, such as large amounts of Wi-Fi points, and maybe rare Pokémon! If you take an interest in competitive battling you won't want to miss them!

Tournaments Rules:

General Rules:

The following ruleset applies to all types of tour competitions in the PS! Wi-Fi room:
  1. Play fair & play respectfully.
  2. Do not timer stall, do not trash talk players.
  3. Use of Hacked Pokemon is strictly banned and has a severe penalty. If you are uncertain about a Pokemon, do not use it.
  4. You must always follow tier & format rules, including custom modifiers when these rules are featured.
  5. Disputes as a result of timer will go as follows, Either; the games choice of who wins on screen as victor is what you both can go by, or if both sides agree one is massively favored over the other in the end state. Always reach out to a helper over timer disputes.
  6. Monthlies are hosted twice the day after the Official Giveaway date just before 2PM EST and 7PM EST accordingly. Participating in more than one will result in forfeiting previously earned prizes.
Failure to comply with the rules will result in disqualification and repeat offenses will result in a ban from participation in any capacity. By participating in our tournaments you accept responsibility for any rule breaks that may occur due to your participation. Be sure to remain in-line with the rules.

Nugget Bridge Guidelines:

  1. All battles take place in-game not on the sim. You may only face each trainer once per month/tier. Make sure to take a picture of the win & lose screen and upload it online for the win report!
  2. Sign up Here!| Report wins Here! | View the Nugget Bridge here! - Don’t forget the number you used to sign up!
  3. You can look for battles in Wi-Fi by saying “LF: Nugget Bridge battle”. Organize the battle in PMs.
  4. Thrown matches, using non-viable teams, and or breaking previously stated rules will result in 0 wp being awarded. Awarded wp and the Nugget Bridge page will update each time a helper checks submitted battles so be patient.
Prizes & Rewards:
Wi-Fi Points (WP) can be used in the Wi-Fi Prize shop and can be earned through all avenues of tournament participation. Check what you could be earning!
  • Daily Simtours - Winner: 3 WP, runner-up: 2 WP, semi-finalist: 1WP.
  • In-game practice tours - Winner: 20 WP runner-up: 15 WP, semi-finalist: 10 WP, all other participants: 5 WP.
  • In-game monthly tours - Winner: 60 WP runner-up: 40 WP, semi-finalist: 30 WP, quater-finalist: 20 WP, all other participants: 10 WP.
  • Nugget Bridge - Winning: 6WP, loss: 3 WP, up to a grand total of 75 WP.
  • Nugget Bridge Breakpoints -Hitting a certain amount of WP during a Nugget Bridge cycle will allow for bonus amounts to be added! At 12: 6 WP extra, 30: 7 WP extra, 61: 14 WP extra.
