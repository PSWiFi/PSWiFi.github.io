Events Overview

Interested in joining our Event Helper team? Great! We are always seeking dedicated individuals to support and contribute to our various event sections. Below is an overview of what being an Event Helper entails.

What is an event helper?

As an Event Helper, you will gain valuable insight into the inner workings of our events and play an active role in their success. You will collaborate with a large, supportive team committed to fostering a welcoming and enjoyable environment for everyone to engage in Pokémon gameplay and activites.

Please note, helpers are limited to assisting with a maximum of two event categories at any given time. This ensures a manageable workload and allows you to fully dedicate yourself to each role. We encourage you to carefully consider your availability before applying, as being an Event Helper requires a meaningful commitment.

Communication and coordination for events primarily take place via Discord. If you do not currently have a Discord account, a staff member will gladly assist you in setting one up upon acceptance. However, participation without Discord access is not possible due to the nature of our event planning.

We invite you to apply for any sections that interest you. A staff member will review your application and respond with a decision or feedback within two weeks. We currently offer opportunities in four helper sections: Small Events, Tournaments, Prizes, and Giveaways. Each section is described in detail below.

Events Types & Descriptions

Small Events Overview

Current Leaders: Argynt, Nicolic & DaWolfKid!

Joining as a Wi-Fi Small Events Helper offers the opportunity to bring your creative event ideas to life for the Scarlet & Violet games.

This flexible role values your imagination, with events ranging from small tournaments to large-scale competitions. Helpers are expected to organize or assist with an event at least once every three months, allowing you to contribute in a way that fits your schedule. If you have an idea but are unsure how to proceed, the Small Events team is here to support and guide you.

There are many event types you can participate in. Some past examples include:

  • Competitive (e.g., Face Off, Treasure Hunt, Digging Contest)
  • Hunting (e.g., Hatching Contest, Secret Santa, Easter Eggs)
  • Creativity (e.g., League Card Contest)
  • Trivia
  • And many more!

Be sure to visit the room regularly and use /rfaq events to stay updated on current events.

Tournaments Overview

Current Leaders: Pooof, Gardevoir&Gallade, Mex & Cosplay Furret!

Being a Wi-Fi Tour Helper requires a moderate amount of commitment; however, it is a way to give back to the people who run your current Tour Events! If you choose to go down the path of many Wi-Fi Event Helpers, you will also get a say inwhat events we host, and you may be able to create your own events if approved!

Besides helping out at the large tournament that happens at the end of every month, what do Tour Helpers do? Plenty!

Nugget Bridge Helper:

Nugget Bridge Helpers verify wins and losses through images submitted by players, and direct users on issues that might occur during a battle, such as disconnection, trouble reporting, timer decisions.

Competitive Pokémon Obtainer:

Competitive Pokémon Obtainers may need to breed, receive events, and catch certain Pokémon we need for the monthly metagame. Once these Pokémon are obtained, they must be trained to be fully battle-ready and given to a Tour Leader.

Sim Tour Helper:

The easiest role in tours; a Sim Tour Helper fosters discussion about that month's tier and then types ~tour leaderboard into the chat to let users practice battling in that tier on Showdown!. Sim Tour Helpers are expected to hype up and supervise their tournament.

Switch Tour Helper:

Possibly the hardest role in tours, however a role which has some room for your creativity; Switch Tour Helpers run our in-game practice tours at times which they can choose. This also means handling forms and documents related to running the tournament.

Giveaways Overview

Current Leaders: Shahafs, WigglyTree & Slug With Swagger!

Wi-Fi Giveaway Helpers are the ones in charge of Wi-Fi's popular official giveaways! This role is a commitment to helping at least once every three months with official giveaways and allows you to have a say in what Pokémon you would like to see given away. What do Giveaway Helpers do? Again, plenty!

Theme Suggestions:

At the start of a giveaway cycle, all helpers are welcome to suggest a theme and a set of Pokémon to go with it. After a period of a few days, themes will be voted on to decide the official theme of that giveaway.

Pokémon Obtainment:

Pokémon for giveaways are obtained entirely within the GA Team. Giveaway Obtainers are responsible for helping to obtain and train rare Pokémon to be given away.

Pokémon Distribution:

On the day of an official giveaway, Giveaway Distributors will sign up for time slots in which to hold lottery giveaways. These giveaways will be recorded on the same spreadsheet as time slots.

Prizeshop Overview

Current Leaders: SexyMalasada & Chimera!

The Wi-Fi Prizes Team is the lifeblood of Wi-Fi events. Made up of individuals who work to provide Pokémon for the Wi-Fi WP Shop, this team serves the important role of making sure there is a wide variety of prizes for event participants.

Helpers will earn WP valuing at one-fifth of each contribution. For example, donating a 100 WP prize will earn the donator 20 WP.
