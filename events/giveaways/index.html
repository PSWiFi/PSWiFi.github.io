Official Giveaway Participation Guidelines:

Want to participate in Official Giveaways? Awesome! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of the monthly Official Wi-Fi Giveaway. information outlining everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators.

Once a month the PS! Wi-Fi room hosts a Giveaway lasting for 12+ hours, featuring over 20 high value Pokemon that you can get your hands on! These giveaways will be held on Sword/Shield or Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl and will occur on the last Saturday of every month, show up, click join and hope the luck is on your side!

Official Giveaway Rules:

General Rules:

  1. Do not double-dip. This means do not join a Giveaway of a Pokemon you have already won in the ongoing giveaway.
  2. Disrespect is not tolerated. Do not make rude/mean comments about ongoing giveaways, we don't want them.
  3. You have 10 minutes to acknowledge a win, and 15 minutes to redeem your prize from the moment you won.
  4. You must have a copy of the game the Official Giveaway is being held in to redeem your prize, no Pokemon HOME redemptions.
  5. You must also have a Nintendo Switch with an active Online subscription.
  6. Have fun! Enjoy yourself, that is what the OGA is all about.
Failure to comply with the rules will result in disqualification and repeat offenses will result in a ban from participation in any capacity. By participating in our tournaments you accept responsibility for any rule breaks that may occur due to your participation. Be sure to remain in-line with the rules.
