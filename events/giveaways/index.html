Official Giveaway Participation Guidelines

Want to participate in Official Giveaways? Awesome! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of the monthly Official Wi-Fi Giveaway. Information outlining everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators.

Once a month the PS! Wi-Fi room hosts a Giveaway lasting for 12+ hours, featuring over 20 high value Pokémon that you can get your hands on! These giveaways will be held on Sword/Shield or Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl and will occur on the last Saturday of every month. All you have to do is show up, click 'Join' and hope the luck is on your side!

Official Giveaway Rules

General Rules:

  1. Do not double-dip. This means do not join a Giveaway of a Pokémon you have previously won in the ongoing giveaway.
    • If you are caught double-dipping, you will be met with a week-long Giveaway Ban which prevents you from joining any Giveaways for two days. Don't miss out on the entire OGA by double-dipping!
  2. Disrespect is not tolerated. Do not make rude or mean comments about ongoing giveaways, we don't want them.
  3. Avoid complaining if you do not win. These giveaways are all luck based, just keep joining!
  4. You have 10 minutes to acknowledge a win, and 15 minutes to redeem your prize from the moment you won.
    • After 15 minutes have passed, you forfeit the prize and you will be met with a week-long Giveaway Ban which prevents you from joining any Giveaways for two days.
  5. You must have a copy of the game the Official Giveaway is being held in to redeem your prize; no Pokémon HOME redemptions.
  6. You must also have a Nintendo Switch with an active Online subscription in order to trade.
  7. Have your OT (Trainer Name)/TID (Trainer ID) or a self-caught Pokémon readily available for the distributor.
  8. Have fun! Enjoy yourself, that is what the OGA is all about. No unofficial giveaways will be hosted on the monthly giveaway day.
Failure to comply with these rules will result in disqualification and repeat offenses will result in a ban from participation in any capacity. By participating in our giveaways you accept responsibility for any rule breaks that may occur due to your participation. Be sure to remain in-line with the rules.
