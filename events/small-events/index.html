Small Events Participation Guidelines:

Want to participate in Small Events? Awesome! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of Small Events and outlines everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators.

Throughout the month, the PS! Wi-Fi room hosts a series of small events with a wide range of variety for you to participate in!

Small Events Rules:

General Rules:

  1. Botting is prohibited in official competitions. This extends to all types of automated play.
  2. Ensure entries are created within the event's timeframe, do not prepare entries in advance. This means you cannot prepare an entry prior to the start date of a new contest or competition.
  3. Forged entries will be disqualified. and room punishment will likely follow.
  4. Two users submitting the same entry outside of team events will be disqualified.
  5. Inappropriate nicknames will be disqualified.
  6. Have fun! Enjoy yourself, that is what small events are all about.
Failure to comply with the rules will result in disqualification and repeat offenses will result in a ban from participation in any capacity. By participating in our small events you accept responsibility for any rule breaks that may occur due to your participation. Be sure to remain in-line with the rules.

Important Information:

  • Small events are a great way to earn amazing Pokemon and Wi-Fi Points that you can spend in the Prize Shop! Collectors won't want to miss them.
  • Each unique event may follow it's own unique ruleset. Be sure to check those out by using the /events view command in the room!
