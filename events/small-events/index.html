Small Events Participation

Want to participate in Small Events? Awesome! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of Small Events and outlines everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators.

Throughout the month, the PS! Wi-Fi room hosts a series of small events with a wide variety of creative ideas for you to participate in!

Small Events Rules
General Rules
  1. Botting is prohibited in official competitions.
    • This extends to all types of automated play to keep competition at a level playing field.
  2. Entries must be created inside the event timeframe.
    • This means you cannot prepare an entry prior to the start date of a new contest or competition.
  3. Forged entries will be disqualified.
    • Use authentic submissions; fake submissions are likely to be met with room punishment.
  4. Two users submitting the same entry outside of team events will be disqualified.
    • This is for obvious reasons, you must make your own individual effort when creating an event submission.
  5. Inappropriate nicknames will be immediately disqualified.
    • Having an inappropriate nickname breaks Global PS! rules and is not acceptable.
  6. In most cases, entries must remain anonymous.
    • For events where entries are private, do not leak your entry to anyone else until the event has concluded.
  7. Do not ask / encourage others to vote towards your entry.
    • Keep it fair! Asking friends to vote for your entry grants you an unfair advantage and also breaks the anonymity rule.
  8. Have fun! Enjoy yourself, that is what small events are all about.
Important Information
  • Small events are a great way to earn amazing Pokémon and Wi-Fi Points that you can spend in the Prize Shop! Collectors won't want to miss them.
  • Each unique event may follow it's own unique ruleset. Be sure to check those out by using the /events view command in the room!
