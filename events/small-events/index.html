Small Events Participation

Want to participate in Small Events? Awesome! This page contains information about rules and guidelines of Small Events and outlines everything you need to know in order to compete! Be sure to read through the whole page carefully as it contains a lot of important information for participators.

Throughout the month, the PS! Wi-Fi room hosts a series of small events with a wide variety of creative ideas for you to participate in!

Small Events Rules:
  1. Botting is prohibited in official competitions. This extends to all types of automated play.

  2. Ensure entries are created within the event's timeframe; do not prepare entries in advance, This means you cannot prepare an entry prior to the start date of a new contest or competition.

  3. Forged entries will be disqualified. Use authentic submissions; fake submissions are likely to be met with room punishment.

  4. Two users submitting the same entry outside of team events will be disqualified.

  5. Inappropriate nicknames will be disqualified. This breaks Global PS! rules and is not acceptable.

  6. Have fun! Enjoy yourself, that is what small events are all about.
