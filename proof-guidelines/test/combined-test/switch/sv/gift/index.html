Proof Checklist:

Unless otherwise stated, everything listed in the checklist below is considered a requirement! Follow along with the checklist to ensure that your album is suitable for trading.

It is recommended to unlock the IV Judge function before attempting to prove a Pokémon, otherwise you will need to prove the IVs from the stats using an IV calculator (or similar tool/application/overlay). You should also have an understanding of how to use the video capture feature or have an external camera ready to take a video.

Scarlet/Violet - Gifts
