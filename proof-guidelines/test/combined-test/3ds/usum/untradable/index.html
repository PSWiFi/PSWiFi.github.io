3DS - Untradable Pokemon

When obtained in 3DS titles, these Pokémon cannot be traded in Wi-Fi under any circumstances.

  • Any Pokémon obtained from Wonder Trade.
  • Shiny OR Legendary/Mythical Pokémon, transferred to Pokémon HOME, that qualify for either the Jumbo OR Teensy mark when transferred to Scarlet/Violet.

Trading any Pokémon in this list will result in room punishment.
