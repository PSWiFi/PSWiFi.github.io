3DS Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in (Ultra) Sun/Moon, Omega Ruby/Alpha Sapphire, and X/Y to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases, your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link. Furthermore, ALL Pokémon must follow the room's requirements for every generation, even if not specifically outlined here. Be sure to review our overall requirements to see if your Pokémon meets the general standard.

As an example, although not directly described on this page, a stationary Pokémon with 6 IVs from an encounter with 3 guaranteed IVs will still need proof because this is a requirement for all generations, as described in our overall proofing rules found above.

It is advised to unlock the IV judge function before attempting to proof a Pokémon.

If you cannot provide the IV judge evaluation you must find an alternative way to prove the IVs of your Pokémon, such as through the teambuilder or other stat calculators.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY Generation 6/7 game:
    • Pokémon obtained from Wild Encounters (including roaming Pokémon).
      • 4 IV+ non-shiny encounters with 0 guaranteed IVs.
      • 5 IV+ Pokémon from Friend Safari or DexNav encounters.
      • [Gen 7] 1% encounter shiny Pokémon from SOS encounters.
      • [Gen 7] 5 IV+ shiny Pokémon from SOS encounters.
      • [Gen 7] 6 IV non-shiny Legendary Pokémon / Ultra Beasts.
    • Pokémon obtained from Events.
      • 3 IV+ shiny or Event Pokémon with 0 guaranteed IVs.
      • 5 IV+ shiny or Event Pokémon with 3 guaranteed IVs.
      • 6 IV non-shiny Event Pokémon.
      • Nicknamed event Pokémon.
    • Pokemon obtained through hatching a shiny Pokémon due to matching TSV values traded between two users require proof.
      • TSV hatched Pokémon must provide a screenshot of the conversation between the two users, agreeing upon a TSV hatch.

Static, Wild, and Roaming Encounters:

Using Overlays or Citra:

For both of these categories, a screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon is necessary. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used (for example, Synchronise, Cute Charm, etc.). Sensitive information such as initial seed and PID/EC can be partially blurred.

You must provide:
  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame with the overlay output visible.
  • A screenshot or picture of the encounter starting (i.e the “Pokémon appeared!” screen) with the output from the overlay clearly visible on-screen.
  • A picture of your Trainer Card/Passport.
  • If 3DSTimeFinder was used to manipulate initial seed on Citra, a screenshot of the tool output that displays frame, seed, time, and date.
Retail RNG & Soft Resetting:
You must provide:
  • A full uncut video starting from a successful capture of your Pokémon leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the stats, nature, ability, and OT/TID.
  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon is necessary. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used (for example, Synchronise, Cute Charm, etc.). Sensitive information such as initial seed and PID/EC can be partially blurred.
  • A picture of your Trainer Card/Passport.

Gift & Mystery Gift Redemptions:

Using Overlays or Citra:
You must provide:
  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame at redemption with the overlay output visible (i.e “You received Pokémon” screen).
  • A screenshot or picture after redemption (i.e “We hope to see you again!” screen or similar) with the output from the overlay clearly visible on-screen displaying the stats and IVs of the redeemed Pokémon.
  • If the event takes your OT/TID you must provide a screenshot of your Trainer Card.
  • If 3DSTimeFinder was used to manipulate initial seed on Citra, a screenshot of the tool output that displays frame, seed, time, and date.
Retail RNG & Soft Resetting:
You must provide:
  • A full uncut video starting from a successful redemption of your Pokémon leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the stats, nature, ability, and OT/TID.
  • If the event takes your OT/TID you must provide a screenshot of your Trainer Card.
