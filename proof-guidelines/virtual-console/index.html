VC Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Red/Blue/Yellow/Green & Gold/Silver/Crystal VC to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

Virtual Console Overall Requirement:

Pokémon obtained in the above mentioned Virtual Console titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

All encounter types:

  • 6 IV Pokémon from PokéTransporter.
  • Shiny Pokémon of any kind.

Virtual Console Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in Virtual Console titles. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Obtained in Red/Blue/Green/Yellow:

If you used an emulator, you must provide:

  • For Wild Encounters a watermarked image of the encounter from the “Player threw the ball” OR “Pokémon Appeared” screen is required.
  • For Gift Redemptions a watermarked image of the Redemption screen is required.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary screen, clearly showing your OT/TID of the Pokémon.
  • If it is a SHINY Pokemon, you will need to provide a picture of PokéTransporter that shows that the Pokémon in question is ready to be picked up.
  • For results obtained with bots/lua scripts, leave the output visible in the screenshots.
  • If this Pokemon was achieved by PokéTransporter RNG abuse, show screenshots of the RNG tool and pcalc overlay used in order to verify this. Sensitive information should be partially blurred (no more than half!)

Note - The watermarked images should clearly show your PS! Username/Handle in order to validate your proof album.

If you used retail hardware/soft resetting, you must provide:

  • For Wild Encounters an image of the encounter from the “Player threw the ball” OR “Pokémon Appeared” screen is required.
  • For Gift Redemptions an image of the Redemption screen is required.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary screen, clearly showing your OT/TID of the Pokémon.
  • If it is a SHINY Pokemon, you will need to provide a picture of PokéTransporter that shows that the Pokémon in question is ready to be picked up.
  • If this Pokemon was achieved by PokéTransporter RNG abuse, show screenshots of the RNG tool and pcalc overlay used in order to verify this. Sensitive information should be partially blurred (no more than half!)

Note - The Encounter/Redemption/Hatch images should be taken using a camera, with a paper slip visible that clearly shows your PS! Username/Handle in order to validate your proof album.
