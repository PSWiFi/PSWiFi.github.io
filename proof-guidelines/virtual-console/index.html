Virtual Console Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in the Red/Blue/Green/Yellow and Gold/Silver/Crystal Virtual Console titles to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases, your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link. Furthermore, ALL Pokémon must follow the room's requirements for every generation, even if not specifically outlined here. Be sure to review our overall requirements to see if your Pokémon meets the general standard.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY Virtual Console game:
  • Shiny Pokémon from all types of encounters.
  • 6 IV Pokémon from all types of encounters.

All Types Of Encounters

You must provide:
  • [Wild Encounter] A picture during an encounter with the Pokémon during the “Player threw the ball” OR “Pokémon Appeared” text box, with a paper slip showing your PS! Username.
  • [Egg / Gift Encounter] A picture during the egg hatch/gift redemption screen with a paper slip showing your PS! Username.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary, clearly showing your OT/TID if not otherwise shown.
  • If the Pokémon was RNG abused with PokéTransporter, show screenshots of the RNG tool and pcalc overlay used in order to verify this.
  • If the Pokémon is a shiny from Gen 1, show it in the PokéTransporter box waiting to be picked up.
  • If your Pokémon was obtained by use of an emulator, you may watermark your images instead of providing a picture with a paper slip.
