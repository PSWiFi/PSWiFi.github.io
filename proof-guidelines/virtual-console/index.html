VC Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Red/Blue/Yellow/Green & Gold/Silver/Crystal VC to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

Virtual Console Overall Requirement:

Pokemon obtained in the above mentioned Virtual Console titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

All encounter types:

  • 6 IV Pokémon from PokéTransporter.
  • Shiny Pokémon of any kind.

Virtual Console Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in Virtual Console titles. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

All Types Of Encounters:

You must provide:

  • [Wild Encounter] A picture during an encounter with the Pokémon during the “Player threw the ball” OR “Pokémon Appeared” text box, with a paper slip showing your PS! Username.
  • [Egg / Gift Encounter] A picture during the egg hatch/gift redemption screen with a paper slip showing your PS! Username.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary, clearly showing your OT/TID if not otherwise shown.
  • If the Pokémon was RNG abused with PokéTransporter, show screenshots of the RNG tool and pcalc overlay used in order to verify this.
  • If the Pokémon is a shiny from Gen 1, show it in the PokéTransporter box waiting to be picked up.
  • If your Pokémon was obtained by use of an emulator, you may watermark your images instead of providing a picture with a paper slip.
