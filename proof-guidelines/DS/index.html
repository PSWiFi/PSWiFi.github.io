DS Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Diamond/Pearl/Platinum, Heart Gold/Soul Silver & Black(2)/White(2) to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

DS Overall Requirement:

Pokemon obtained in the above mentioned DS titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

Pokémon obtained from Wild/Roaming/Stationary/Gift encounters:

Encounters with 0 guaranteed IVs
  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon
Encounters with 3 guaranteed IVs
  • 5 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 6 IV non-shiny Pokémon

Pokémon obtained from eggs:

  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon

Pokémon obtained from event distributions:

Distributions with 0 guaranteed IVs
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Distributions with 3 guaranteed IVs
  • 6 IV non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Other
  • Nicknamed Pokémon from event distributions as a result of matching trainer information
  • "Sometimes shiny" shiny event Pokémon
  • ALL "sometimes shiny when traded" event Pokémon from eggs. (e.g Manaphy)

Shiny Pokémon obtained from TSV hatches:

  • Pokémon obtained through hatching a shiny Pokémon due to matching TSV values traded between two users require proof.
  • TSV hatched Pokémon must provide a screenshot of the conversation between the two users, agreeing upon a TSV hatch.

Gen 4 & 5 Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in Gen 4 and Gen 5 (DS) titles. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Stationary, Wild, Egg & Gift Encounters:

If you are using emulator with .lua scripts you must provide:

  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame with the .lua script output visible.

  • A screenshot or picture of the encounter starting (i.e the “Pokémon appeared!” screen for encounters & "You received an egg!" screen for eggs) with the output from the overlay clearly visible on-screen.

  • A screenshot or picture of the Pokemon summary, displaying the stats and OT/TID of the Pokemon that was obtained. You may use the lua script to confirm the IVs of the Pokémon.

  • A screenshot or picture of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used. Sensitive information such as Seed/PID/EC can be partially blurred.

  • For Gen 5 RNG using C-Gear, show a picture or screenshot of hitting your C-gear seed.

  • For roaming encounters, you must show the frame being hit at the correct screen that the roamer is generated on.

For soft resetting and retail RNG abuse you must provide:

  • A full uncut video starting from a successful capture of your Pokémon leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the stats, nature, ability, and OT/TID.

  • A screenshot or picture of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used. Sensitive information such as Seed/PID/EC can be partially blurred.

Mystery Gift Redemptions:

If you are using emulator with .lua scripts you must provide:

  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame with the .lua script output visible.

  • A screenshot or picture after redemption (i.e “You received a Pokémon!” screen or similar) with the output from the lua script clearly visible on-screen displaying the stats and IVs of the redeemed Pokémon.

  • A screenshot or picture of the Pokemon summary, displaying the stats and OT/TID of the Pokemon that was obtained. You may use the lua script to confirm the IVs of the Pokémon.

  • A screenshot or picture of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used. Sensitive information such as Seed/PID/EC can be partially blurred.

  • If the event takes your OT/TID, a picture of your Trainer Card showing your OT/TID combination. This includes event eggs that inherit the OT/TID from the hatcher.

For soft resetting and retail RNG abuse you must provide:

  • A full uncut video starting from a successful redemption of your Pokémon in a Pokecenter leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the stats, nature, ability, and OT/TID.

  • A screenshot or picture of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used. Sensitive information such as Seed/PID/EC can be partially blurred.

  • If the event takes your OT/TID, a picture of your Trainer Card showing your OT/TID combination. This includes event eggs that inherit the OT/TID from the hatcher.
