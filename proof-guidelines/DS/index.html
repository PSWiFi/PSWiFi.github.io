DS Proofing Guidelines:

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Diamond/Pearl/Platinum, HeartGold/SoulSilver and Black(2)/White(2) to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY generation 4/5 game:
  • 3IV+ Shiny Pokémon from all encounter types - Wild/Stationary/Roaming/Egg.
  • 4IV Pokémon from all encounter types - Wild/Stationary/Roaming/Egg.
  • 3IV+ Pokémon from event distributions with no guaranteed IVs.
  • 4IV+ Pokémon from event distributions with 2+ guaranteed IVs.
  • “Sometimes Shiny” Shiny event Pokémon.

Static, Wild and Roaming Encounters:

RNG using .lua scripts:
You must provide:
  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame with the .lua seed output visible.
  • A screenshot or picture of the encounter starting (i.e. The “Pokémon appeared!” screen with the output from the lua script clearly visible on-screen.
  • Screenshots or pictures of the summary screen clearly displaying the stats and OT/TID of the Pokémon.
  • You must also provide a screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon, you should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used (Synchronise, Cute Charm, etc.).
  • For Gen 5 RNG using C-Gear, show a picture or screenshot of hitting your C-gear seed.
  • For roaming encounters you must show the frame being hit at the correct screen that the roamer is generated on.
Retail RNG & Soft Resetting:
You must provide:
  • A full uncut video from hitting your target Pokémon starting from the “Pokémon appeared!” screen in-battle. The video should contain a successful capture showing off all the relevant screens (Summary, OT/TID)
  • You must show the RNG tool used either in an attached screenshot or during the video capture. This must include all your specific configurations (Synchronise, Cute Charm, Method).

Gift & Mystery Gift Redemptions:

RNG using .lua scripts:
You must provide:
  • A screenshot or picture hitting your target frame with the .lua seed output visible. (i.e the “You redeemed Pokémon” screen).
  • Screenshots or pictures of the summary screen clearly displaying the stats and OT/TID of the Pokémon.
  • You must also provide a screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon, you should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.
  • If the Pokémon takes your OT/TID you must provide a picture of your trainer card.
Retail RNG & Soft Resetting:
You must provide:
  • A full uncut video from hitting your target frame starting from the “You redeemed a Pokémon” screen. The video should contain a successful redemption showing off all the relevant screens (Summary, OT/TID)
  • You must show the RNG tool used either in an attached screenshot or during the video capture. This must include all your specific configurations (Synchronise, Cute Charm, Method, etc).
