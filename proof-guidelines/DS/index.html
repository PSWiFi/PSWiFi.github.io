DS Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Diamond/Pearl/Platinum, Heart Gold/Soul Silver & Black(2)/White(2) to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

DS Overall Requirement:

Pokémon obtained in the above mentioned DS titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

Pokémon obtained from Wild/Roaming/Static Encounters & Egg/Gift Redemptions:

  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon

Pokémon obtained from Event Distributions:

Distributions with 0 guaranteed IVs
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Distributions with 2+ guaranteed IVs
  • 6 IV non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Other
  • Nicknamed Event Pokémon made possible by RNGing matching trainer information
  • "Sometimes Shiny" Shiny Event Pokémon (e.g Kyushu Trains Events)
  • ALL Shiny Egg Event Pokémon made possible to hatch as shiny exclusively via trading. (e.g Manaphy Egg)

Gen 4 & 5 Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in Gen 4 and Gen 5 (DS) titles. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Obtained in Diamond/Pearl/Platinum or HeartGold/SoulSilver:

If using DeSmuME/BizHawk or a similar app with .lua scripts for RNG abuse you must provide:

  • For Encounters, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e Sweet Scent animation for Wild; or Before last A press/movement for Statics) and a screenshot of the encounter initiation (i.e the “Pokémon appeared!” screen or similar).

  • For Redemptions, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e “Player received Pokémon!” screen) and a screenshot of the summary screens cleary showing OT/TID, Nature and IVs.

  • For Eggs, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e “Do you want it? Y/N” screen) and a screenshot immediately after recieving the egg confirming the stats and internal details.
{% include tooltips/overlays.html %} or lua script output must be visible in all of the above mentioned screenshots to prove the correct target frame has been hit.
  • A screenshot of the Pokémon Summary displaying both the stats and OT/TID of the Pokémon that was obtained (preferred) and/or your Trainer Card.

For Events that take the Obtainer or Hatcher's OT/TID (including traded Eggs), a Trainer Card screenshot is mandatory!
  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.
    {% include common/sensitive-info-paragraph.html %}

We recommend using the most recent Luas for your RNGs so as to show & prove the IVs of your Pokémon, in addition to the Seed in the screenshots mentioned above. If your Lua does not display the correct IVs/Nature of the Pokémon, Summary screenshots showcasing stats are considered mandatory.

For soft resetting and retail RNG abuse you must provide:

  • A full uncut video starting from a successful capture or redemption of your Pokémon, leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the Stats, Nature, Ability, and OT/TID.

  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.
    {% include common/sensitive-info-paragraph.html %}

Obtained in Black/White or Black 2/White 2:

  • For Redemptions, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e “Good day. You must be Player.” screen) and a screenshot of the summary screens cleary showing OT/TID, Nature and IVs.
    For Dream Radar specifically, a screenshot immediately after redemption is manadatory (i.e “Research data was written...” screen).

  • For Eggs, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e “Do you want it? Y/N” screen) and a screenshot immediately after recieving the egg confirming the stats and internal details.

For Events that take the Obtainer or Hatcher's OT/TID (including traded Eggs), a Trainer Card screenshot is mandatory!
  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.

  • For RNG with C-Gear Seeds (e.g BW Entralink RNG), a screenshot of you hitting your C-Gear Seed.

We recommend using the most recent Luas for your RNGs so as to show & prove the IVs of your Pokémon, in addition to the Seed in the screenshots mentioned above. If your Lua does not display the correct IVs/Nature of the Pokémon, Summary screenshots showcasing stats are considered mandatory.
