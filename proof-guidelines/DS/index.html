DS Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Diamond/Pearl/Platinum, Heart Gold/Soul Silver & Black(2)/White(2) to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

DS Overall Requirement:

Pokemon obtained in the above mentioned DS titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

Pokémon obtained from Wild/Roaming/Static Encounters & Egg/Gift Redemptions:

  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon

Pokémon obtained from Event Distributions:

Distributions with 0 guaranteed IVs
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Distributions with 2+ guaranteed IVs
  • 6 IV non-shiny/shiny Pokémon
Other
  • Nicknamed Event Pokémon made possible by RNGing matching trainer information
  • "Sometimes Shiny" Shiny Event Pokémon (e.g Kyushu Trains Events)
  • ALL Shiny Egg Event Pokémon made possible to hatch as shiny exclusively via trading. (e.g Manaphy Egg)

Gen 4 & 5 Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in Gen 4 and Gen 5 (DS) titles. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Static, Wild & Roaming Encounters / Egg, Gift & Event Redemptions:

If using Desmume or a similar app with .lua scripts for RNG abuse you must provide:

  • For Encounters, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e at Sweet Scent animation for Wild; Before last A press or movement for Statics; and Before Roamer generation for Roamers).
    Also a screenshot of the Encounter starting (i.e “Pokémon appeared!”).
    The lua output must be clearly visible on-screen for all screenshots.

  • For Redemptions, a screenshot of you hitting your target frame (i.e “Take good care of it.” or “Do you Want it? Y/N” screen for Eggs & Gifts & “Player received Pokemon!” or “Good day. You must be Player.” screens for Events in Gen 4 & Gen 5 respectively).
    Also a screenshot after Redemption of the Egg/Gift or Event mon (i.e “Player received Egg/Pokemon..” or “We look forward to your next visit!” screens respectively).
    The lua output must be clearly visible on-screen for all screenshots.

  • A screenshot of the Pokemon Summary displaying both the stats and OT/TID of the Pokemon that was obtained (preferred) or your Trainer Card.
    In the case of Event Redemptions, or if an older Lua that doesn't show IVs was used, Summary screenshots are mandatory!
    For Events that take the Obtainer or Hatcher's OT/TID (including traded Eggs), a Trainer Card screenshot is also mandatory!

  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.
    Sensitive information such as Seeds & PID can be partially blurred on every screenshot (no more than half!).

  • For Gen 5 RNG with C-Gear Seeds (e.g BW Entralink RNG), a screenshot of you hitting your C-Gear Seed.

We recommend using the most recent Luas for your RNGs so as to show & prove the IVs of your Pokémon, in addition to the Seed in the screenshots mentioned above.

For soft resetting and retail RNG abuse you must provide:

  • A full uncut video starting from a successful capture or redemption of your Pokémon, leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the Stats, Nature, Ability, and OT/TID.

  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.
    Sensitive information such as Seeds & PID can be partially blurred on every screenshot (no more than half!).
