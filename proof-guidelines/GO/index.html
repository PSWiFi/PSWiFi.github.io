Pokémon GO! Proofing Guidelines:

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Pokémon GO to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Pokémon GO:
  • Shiny Mythical Pokémon.
  • 5IV+ Shiny Pokémon.
  • 6IV Legendary and Mythical Pokémon.

All Types Of Encounters

You must provide:
  • A picture of your Pokémon GO Username/IGN.
  • A picture proving the IVs of the Pokémon while in GO.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary screen in HOME after transfer, this must also display matching and accurate IVs (this can be on HOME Switch or HOME Mobile versions).
  • A picture of the Pokémon OT/TID if not otherwise obvious.
