Pokémon GO! Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Pokémon GO to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases, your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link. Furthermore, ALL Pokémon must follow the room's requirements for every generation, even if not specifically outlined here. Be sure to review our overall requirements to see if your Pokémon meets the general standard.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Pokémon GO:
  • Shiny Mythical Pokémon.
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon.
  • 6 IV Legendary/Mythical Pokémon.

All Types Of Encounters

You must provide:
  • A picture of your Pokémon GO Username/IGN.
  • A picture proving the IVs of the Pokémon while in GO. This can be on the main summary page of the Pokémon, or a screenshot of a filtered search.
  • A picture of the Pokémon summary screen in HOME after transfer. This must display matching and accurate IVs (this can be done on HOME Switch or HOME Mobile versions).
  • A picture of the Pokémon OT/TID if not otherwise obvious.
