GBA Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Fire Red/Leaf Green & Ruby/Sapphire/Emerald to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases, your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link. Furthermore, ALL Pokémon must follow the room's requirements for every generation, even if not specifically outlined here. Be sure to review our overall requirements to see if your Pokémon meets the general standard.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

Pokémon from in-life event distributions, such as Wish Chansey, cannot be traded in the PS! Wi-Fi Room.

Static, Wild and Roaming Encounters:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY generation 3 game:
  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon from all encounter types.
  • 4 IV+ Pokémon from all encounter types.
  • Shiny Wishmaker Jirachi.
RNG using .lua scripts:
You must provide:
  • A screenshot or picture of the encounter starting (i.e the “Pokémon appeared!” screen with the output from the lua script clearly visible on-screen.
  • Screenshots or pictures of the summary screen clearly displaying the stats of the Pokémon, you may also include a picture of the OT/TID screen.
    • If you did not provide a picture of the OT/TID screen, you must show a picture of your trainer card instead.
  • For roaming encounters, you must show the frame being hit at the correct screen that the roamer is generated on. (i.e selecting the colour on the TV in the player's home for Latias and Latios)
  • You must also provide a screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon, you should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used (such as Synchronise, Cute Charm, etc.).
Retail RNG & Soft Resetting:
You must provide:
  • A full uncut video from hitting your target Pokémon starting from the “Pokémon appeared!” screen in-battle. The video should contain a successful capture showing off all the relevant screens such as the summary page and OT/TID.
  • You must also provide a screenshot of the tool used to RNG the Pokémon, you should show your seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used (such as Synchronise, Cute Charm, etc.).
  • An image of your trainer card.
