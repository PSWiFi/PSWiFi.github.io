Legends: Z-A - Untradable Pokemon

When obtained in Legends: Z-A, these Pokémon cannot be traded in Wi-Fi under any circumstances.

  • 6IV Pokémon from in-life event distributions.
  • 5IV+ Shiny Alpha Fossils from the fossil revivial NPC.
  • 6IV Non-Shiny Alpha Fossils from the fossil revivial NPC.

Trading any Pokémon in this list will result in room punishment.
