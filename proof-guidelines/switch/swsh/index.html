When obtained in Sword/Shield, any Pokémon that meet the conditions of this list requires proof when trading.

Encounters & Gifts with less than 3 guaranteed IVs
  • 4 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 5 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon
Encounters & Gifts with more than 3 guaranteed IVs
  • 5 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 6 IV or flawless non-shiny Pokémon
Max Raid Encounters
  • 6 IV shiny Pokémon
  • 6 IV non-shiny Pokémon
Mystery Gifts
  • 4 IV+ Pokémon from distributions with less than 3 guaranteed IVs
  • 6 IV Pokémon from distributions with more than 3 guaranteed IVs
Other/Misc
  • Shiny Pokémon that qualify for the Jumbo or Teensy mark when transferred to Scarlet/Violet
