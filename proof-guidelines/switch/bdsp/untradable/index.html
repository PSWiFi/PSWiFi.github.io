Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl - Untradable Pokemon

When obtained in Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl, these Pokémon cannot be traded in Wi-Fi under any circumstances.

  • 6IV Pokémon from in-life event distributions.
  • 6IV shiny Pokémon from eggs obtained in Link Trades.
  • Any Pokémon obtained from Surprise Trade.

Trading any Pokémon in this list will result in room punishment.
