Switch Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Let's Go, Sword/Shield, Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl, and Legends: Arceus to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link.

It is advised to unlock the IV judge function before attempting to prove a Pokémon.

If you cannot provide the IV judge evaluation you must find an alternative way to prove the IVs of your Pokémon, such as Teambuilder or other stat calculators.

In Legends: Arceus you must show off the effort levels of your Pokémon immediately after the initial encounter instead of an IV judge evaluation.

When a Pokémon is sent to boxes:
  • In Sword/Shield it will go to the first available box slot in the box you last viewed.
  • In Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl it will go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
  • In Legends: Arceus it will also go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
  • In Let's Go it will also go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
In Sword/Shield and Legends: Arceus, Overworld spawns will be the same until you leave the area, this means you can save and retry if you make a mistake.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY generation 8 game:
  • 5IV+ Shiny Pokémon with 3+ guaranteed IVs.
  • 6IV Pokémon with 3+ guaranteed IVs.
  • 3IV+ Pokémon with 0 guaranteed IVs.
  • 3IV+ Global Mystery Gift redemptions with 0 guaranteed IVs.
  • 5IV+ Global Mystery Gift redemptions with 3 guaranteed IVs.
  • Pokémon from unique code event distribution methods (e.g Fiesta Snorlax).
The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Let's Go Pikachu & Eevee:
  • Shiny Pokémon from 1% or less chance overworld spawns.
  • Shiny Articuno/Zapdos/Moltres from overworld spawns.
  • Mew from the Pokéball+.
The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Sword & Shield:
  • 5IV+ Shiny Pokémon from Max Raid Battles or Dynamax Adventures.
  • Shiny Pokémon with the “Rare Mark”.
  • Shiny Sinistea-Antique from a Wild encounter.
The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl:
  • 5IV+ Shiny Pokémon from PokeRadar chains.
The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in Legends: Arceus:
  • 5IV+ Shiny Pokémon from all encounter types.
  • Flawless Legendary/Mythical Pokémon.
The following Pokémon/Encounter types are untradable:
  • (Pokémon hatched from) eggs obtained in Link Trades.
  • 6IV Pokémon from in-life event distributions.
  • Shiny Pokémon from Watchtower Max Raid distributions (SwSh).
  • 6IV Pokémon from Legends: Arceus (No sufficient proofing methods).

Sword & Shield Proofing:

Max Raid Battles & Dynamax Adventure:
  • You must provide a consistent video of a successful capture leading into in-box IV evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature. The video should start on or before the raid reward screen.
  • If your raid was obtained using raid RNG, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.

Stationary, Wild & Gift Encounters:
  • You must provide a consistent video of a successful capture or redemption leading into in-box IV evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature.
  • Pokémon obtained from Overworld RNG abuse should have a screenshot of the RNG tool used accompanying the proof video.
  • Eternatus is a unique case and an exception, your video portion can start from any portion of the cutscene and most provide all above information. It is impossible to have the IV judge evaluation so you may prove the IVs using a Teambuilder screenshot or similar.
  • Pokémon obtained from brilliant aura and chain fishing have a higher chance to have multiple perfect IVs, so be prepared to make proof for these Pokémon properly as and when they appear.
  • Wild shiny Pokemon with a Rare Mark can appear at any time! Be sure to check the Ribbon summary of any shiny you catch!
  • Rare 1% Wild Encounter Shinies can appear at any time! So be prepared to make proof for these Pokémon properly as and when they appear.

Mystery Gift Redemptions:
  • You must show a picture of the redemption screen, clearly showing a valid code has been used, along with a picture clearly displaying the IVs and nature of the redeemed Pokémon.
  • If the event Pokémon takes the OT/TID of the player (e.g Pokéball+ Mew in LGPE), you must show a picture of your save screen, displaying your IGN as well as the OT/TID summary screens of the Pokémon.
  • All pictures require a paper slip on display clearly showing your PS! Username.

Legends: Arceus Proofing:

Overworld, Legendary & Mythical Encounters:
  • You must provide a consistent video of a successful capture or redemption leading into in-box Effort Level evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature.
  • Your Pokémon must be sent to the party. Ensure you have a slot available for it to go to.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved by RNG abuse, you can include screenshots of tools used to further prove IVs. You may also use overlays such as capturesight accompanied with a proof video to prove the IVs.
Legends: Arceus Example

You can turn off autosave to re-encounter most Pokémon in the overworld. You can use this to your advantage and save upon encountering any Pokémon you believe may need proof, and you can then adjust your party accordingly.

Due to the complex Effort Level system, only full 31 IVs will be considered towards the total IV count proof barrier. This will be able to be displayed after initial capture as an initial effort level of 3 indicates a perfect IV of 31 in the stat.
