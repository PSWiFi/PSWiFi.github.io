Switch Proofing Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Sword/Shield, Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl, Let's Go Pikachu/Eevee & Legends: Arceus to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases, your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link. Furthermore, ALL Pokémon must follow the room's requirements for every generation, even if not specifically outlined here. Be sure to review our overall requirements to see if your Pokémon meets the general standard.

As an example, although not directly described on this page, a stationary Pokémon with 6 IVs from an encounter with 3 guaranteed IVs will still need proof because this is a requirement for all generations, as described in our overall proofing rules found above.

It is advised to unlock the IV judge function before attempting to prove a Pokémon.

If you cannot provide the IV judge evaluation you must find an alternative way to prove the IVs of your Pokémon, such as Teambuilder or other stat calculators.

In Legends: Arceus you must show off the effort levels of your Pokémon immediately after the initial encounter instead of an IV judge evaluation.

When a Pokémon is sent to boxes:
  • In Sword/Shield it will go to the first available box slot in the box you last viewed.
  • In Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl it will go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
  • In Legends: Arceus it will also go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
  • In Let's Go it will also go into the first available box slot starting from box 1.
In Sword/Shield and Legends: Arceus, Overworld spawns will be the same until you leave the area, this means you can save and retry if you make a mistake.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

All Generation 8 Titles:
  • 3 IV+ Pokémon from event distribution codes with 0 guaranteed IVs.
  • 5 IV+ Pokémon from event distribution codes with 3 guaranteed IVs.
  • Shiny Pokémon from 1% chance encounters.
Event Pokémon, such as HOME distributions, with fixed IVs/shininess do not need proof.

Pokemon hatched from eggs do not need proof, eggs that were obtained via link trades and then hatched are banned from trading in the room.

Sword/Shield Specifics:
  • Shiny Pokémon from a Wild Encounter with a Rare Mark.
  • Wild Shiny Sinistea-Antique.
  • 3 IV+ Shiny Pokémon from a Wild Encounter.
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon from Max Raid Dens or Dynamax Adventures.
  • 6 IV Pokémon from Max Raid Dens or Dynamax Adventures.
  • 6 IV Pokémon from promotional Max Raid Dens with a boosted shiny chance.
Brilliant Diamond/Shining Pearl Specifics:
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon from PokéRadar chains.
  • 6 IV Pokémon from PokéRadar chains.
Let's Go! Pikachu/Let's Go! Eevee Specifics:
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon from Catch Combo chains.
  • 6 IV Pokémon from Catch Combo chains.
  • Wild Shiny Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres from Overworld-based encounters.
Legends: Arceus Specifics:
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon from encounters in the Overworld, Space-Time Distortions, or (Massive) Mass Outbreaks.
  • Flawless Legendary and Mythical Pokémon (flawless is considered perfect 5 IV+ in Legends: Arceus).
The following Pokémon/Encounter types are untradable:
  • Pokémon hatched from eggs obtained in Link Trades.
  • 6IV Pokémon from in-life event distributions.
  • Shiny Pokémon from Watchtower Max Raid distributions (SwSh).
  • 6IV Pokémon from Legends: Arceus (No sufficient proofing methods).

Sword & Shield Proofing:

Max Raid Battles & Dynamax Adventure:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture leading into in-box IV evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature. The video should start on or before the raid reward screen.
  • If your raid was obtained using RAID RNG, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.

Stationary, Wild & Gift Encounters:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture or redemption leading into in-box IV evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature of the Pokémon you caught.
  • If your encounter was obtained using OVERWORLD RNG, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.
  • Wild Shiny Pokemon can appear at any time! Be prepared to proof them as and when they show up. Pokemon with Brilliant Aura have a higher chance to have multiple perfect IVs.
  • Eternatus is a unique case and an exception. Your video portion can start from any portion of the cutscene and most provide all above information. It is impossible to have the IV judge evaluation so you may prove the IVs using a Teambuilder screenshot or similar.

Brilliant Diamond & Shining Pearl Proofing:

Stationary, Wild & Gift Encounters:
Video Proofing:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture or redemption leading into in-box IV evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature of the Pokémon.
  • Pokémon obtained from RNG abuse should have a screenshot of the RNG tool used accompanying the proof video. Sensitive information can be partially blurred.
Screenshot Proof (Using CFW Overlays):
You must provide:
  • For Stationary/Wild encounters, you should include screenshots from the overlay page that clearly displays the IVs, EC and PID of your Pokémon during battle.
  • For in-game gift redemptions, you should include screenshots from the overlay page that clearly displays the IVs, EC and PID of your Pokémon immediately after redemption.
  • In all cases you should include screenshots from the same overlay page on the summary screen of your Pokémon, clearly showing your OT/TID.
  • Pokémon obtained from RNG abuse should have a screenshot of the RNG tool used accompanying the overlay screenshots. Sensitive information should be partially blurred.

Legends: Arceus Proofing:

Due to the complexity of the Effort Level system, only full 31 IVs are counted towards the IV proof barrier.

Overworld & Outbreak Encounters:
You must provide:
  • A consistent video of a successful capture, leading into in-box Effort Level evaluation and summary screens, enough to clearly show OT/TID and nature of the Pokémon.
  • Your Pokémon must be sent to the party. Ensure you have a slot available for it to go to. This is due to the the lack of a remote-access box system.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved by RNG abuse, you should include screenshots of tools used to further prove IVs. You may also use overlays such as capturesight accompanied with a proof video to prove the IVs.
  • Disable autosave to re-encounter most Pokémon in the overworld. Use this to your advantage and save upon encountering any Pokémon you believe may need proof, and you can then adjust your party accordingly.

Mystery Gift Proofing:

Unless otherwise stated, the following rules apply for Mystery Gifts in all Switch titles.

Video Proofing:
You must provide:
  • A video of the redemption, clearly showing a valid code has been used, the video must clearly display the IVs and nature of the Pokémon in the box immediately after redemption.
  • If the event Pokémon takes the OT/TID of the player, you must show a picture of your trainer card or save screen, as well as the OT/TID summary screens of the Pokémon.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved using RNG abuse, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.
  • A paper slip on display in your videos clearly showing your PS! Username. (Watermark is acceptable for video proofing.)
Mystery Gift Video Example

Image Proofing:
You must provide:
  • A picture of the redemption screen, clearly showing a valid code has been used, along with a picture clearly displaying the IVs and nature of the redeemed Pokémon.
  • If the event Pokémon takes the OT/TID of the player, you must show a picture of your trainer card or save screen, as well as the OT/TID summary screens of the Pokémon.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved using RNG abuse, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.
  • A paper slip on display in your images clearly showing your PS! Username. (This means you will need to use an external camera to take the pictures.)
Mystery Gift Picture Example

Screenshot Proofing (Using CFW Overlays - BDSP only):
You must provide:
  • A screenshot of the redemption screen showing a valid code has been used, with the overlay visible clearly displaying the IVs and nature of the redeemed Pokémon.
  • A screenshot of the in-box summary screen showing the OT/TID of the redeemed Pokémon, with the overlay visible again clearly displaying the IVs and nature of the redeemed Pokémon.
  • If your Pokémon was achieved using RNG abuse, you must provide a screenshot of the tool used displaying your seed, frame count, IVs, PID and EC. It is encouraged to partially blur the seed, PID and EC.
  • For the BDSP Manaphy egg specifically, you must provide an uncut video of the egg hatching, leading into IV judge evaluation and showing the summary pages. (It's possible to save before hatching the egg!)
