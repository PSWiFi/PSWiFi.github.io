When obtained in Pokémon GO, any Pokémon that meet the conditions of this list requires proof when trading.

From any type of encounter (wild/raid/research/etc.):
  • All Shiny Mythical Pokémon from Research Tasks.
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Mythical Pokémon from Raids.
  • 5 IV+ Shiny Pokémon of any kind.
  • 6 IV Legendary/Mythical Pokémon.
Meltan/Melmetal are included in the list of Mythical Pokémon. Note - Be prepared to provide proof for any GO Pokémon from the above list that has both 15 Attack and 15 Defence IVs. When transferred to HOME, its Speed IV is generated upon entry and may roll a significant value, making proof necessary. {% include proof-accordion/bottom.html %}
