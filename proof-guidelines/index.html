Proof Guidelines

A checklist for creating & verifying a proof album in any game.

Certain Pokémon require proof before being traded. The requirements vary based on the game, and encounter method. You can check below if your Pokémon needs proof, and what you need to include in your album.

Select a Console, Game, and Encounter Type below to view the requirements.
