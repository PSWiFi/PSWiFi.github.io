GameCube Proof Guidelines

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Colo/XD, Channel, and the Colosseum Bonus Discs to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi. Only information for the above mentioned games is provided on this page.

GameCube Overall Requirement:

Pokémon obtained in the above mentioned GameCube titles that meet the description of anything listed directly below need proof to trade inside the PS! Wi-Fi room.

Pokémon obtained from Trainer Battles or Gifts:

  • 3 IV+ shiny Pokémon
  • 4 IV+ non-shiny Pokémon

Other:

  • Shiny Wishmaker Jirachi
  • Shiny Channel Jirachi

Colosseum & XD Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained Colosseum/XD. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Trainer Battles & Gift Encounters:

If using Dolphin or a similar app with .lua/RAM Watch scripts you must provide:

  • A screenshot of your trainer card with your current Seed on display, along with the RNG tool proving you're close to your target frame (Lua scripts and RAM watches are acceptable).

  • A screenshot in-battle with your target (clearly showing PID if Lua scripts and RAM watches are used).

  • A screenshot of your Pokémon summary screen after purification (you may use a Lua script or alternative source to prove the IVs).

  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.

For soft resetting and retail RNG abuse you must provide:

  • A full uncut video starting from a successful capture or redemption of your Pokémon, leading into the in-box summary screens clearly showing the Stats, Nature, Ability, and OT/TID.

  • A screenshot of the tool used to RNG abuse the Pokémon. You must show your Seed and desired target frame along with any other specific configurations used.

Bonus Disc Proofing

This section of the page covers how to create proof for Pokémon obtained in GameCube Bonus Discs. In all cases, your PS! Username must be visible somewhere within your proof link so that we can easily verify exactly where the Pokémon has come from.

Obtained from bonus discs:

For Ageto Celebi you must provide:

  • A screenshot of the Celebi redemption screen with your current Seed on display, along with the RNG tool proving you are close to your target frame.

  • A screenshot of the Celebi being redeemed and sent to a GBA from the Colosseum Bonus Disc with some identifying information (such as your PS! username) visible.

  • A screenshot of your Celebi summary screens in a Gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use Lua scripts to prove the IVs).

For Wishmaker Jirachi you must provide:

  • A screenshot of your current save time with the Wishmaker Jirachi .lua script visible on screen, displaying your Seed, delay and target time.

  • A screenshot from a Seed searcher (such as Wishmaker-calc or XD Seed) clearly showing that you have a valid rschecksum.

  • A screenshot of the Jirachi being redeemed and sent to a GBA from the Colosseum Bonus Disc with some identifying information (such as your PS! username) visible.

  • A screenshot of your Jirachi summary screens in a Gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use Lua scripts to prove the IVs).

For Channel Jirachi you must provide:

  • A screenshot of the Jirachi “Are you ready?” redemption screen clearly showing your current Seed.

  • A screenshot of the RNG tool showing you are close to your target spread along with your current Seed, and target frame.

  • A screenshot of the “You got Jirachi!” screen on GBA, clearly showing your RNG tool, desired Jirachi spread, and PS! username on screen.

  • A screenshot of your Jirachi summary screens in a Gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use Lua scripts to prove the IVs).
