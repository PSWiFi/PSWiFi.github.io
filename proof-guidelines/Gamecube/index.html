GameCube Proofing Guidelines:

This page contains information and examples for how to create proof for your Pokémon in Colosseum, XD Gale of Darkness, Channel & Gamecube bonus discs to an acceptable standard for general trading in PS! Wi-Fi.

In all cases your PS! Username should be visible somewhere within your proof link.

Minimum Proof Requirement:

The following Pokémon/Encounter types need proof when obtained in ANY generation 3 game:
  • 3IV+ Shiny Pokémon from all encounter types.
  • 4IV Pokémon from all encounter types.
  • Shiny Wishmaker Jirachi.

Colosseum & XD Gale of Darkness

RNG using .lua scripts or RAM watch:
You must provide:
  • A picture of your trainer card with your current seed on display, along with the RNG tool proving you’re close to your target frame (Lua scripts and RAM watches are acceptable).
  • A picture in battle with your target (clearly showing PID if Lua scripts and RAM watches are used).
  • A picture of your Pokémon summary screen after purification (you may use a Lua script or alternative source to prove the IVs).

Bonus Disc Pokémon

Ageto Celebi:
You must provide:
  • A picture of the Celebi redemption screen with your current seed on display, along with the RNG tool proving you are close to your target frame.
  • A picture of the Celebi being redeemed and sent to a GBA from the Colosseum Bonus Disc with some identifying information (such as your PS! username) visible.
  • A picture of your Celebi summary screens in a Gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use .lua scripts to prove the IVs).
Celebi Bonus Disc Example

Wishmaker Jirachi:
You must provide:
  • A picture of your current save time with the Wishmaker Jirachi .lua script visible on screen, displaying your seed, delay and target time.
  • A picture from a seed searcher (such as Wishmaker-calc or XD Seed) clearly showing that you have a valid rschecksum.
  • A picture of the Jirachi being redeemed and sent to a GBA from the Colosseum Bonus Disc with some identifying information (such as your PS! username) visible.
  • A picture of your Jirachi summary screens in a gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use .lua scripts to prove the IVs).
Jirachi Bonus Disc Example

Channel Jirachi:
You must provide:
  • A picture of the Jirachi “Are you ready?” redemption screen clearly showing your current seed.
  • A picture of the RNG tool showing you are close to your target spread along with your current seed, and target frame.
  • A picture of the “You got Jirachi” screen on GBA, clearly showing your RNG tool, desired Jirachi spread and PS username on screen.
  • Show a picture of your Jirachi summary screens in a Gen 3 game, clearly showing stats and nature (you may use Lua scripts to prove the IVs).
